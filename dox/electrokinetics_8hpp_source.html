92 /* Constants enumerating the links of a node in the link flux system EK_LINK_xyz
93  is the number of the link in direction (x, y, z), where x, y and z can be 0,
94  U or D representing 0 and one agrid in direction of or against the x, y or z
95  axis. The numbering differs from the one used in the LB since the LB
96  velocities are directed but the links are not. Links 0 - 8 represent
97  the odd LB velocities and numbers 13 - 21 represent the even LB velocities
98  (without the 0). In between there are the links connecting the corners, which
99  represent the 3rd shell not used in the LB but in the advection. The
100  following 13 constants are only defined for the sake of completeness.*/
