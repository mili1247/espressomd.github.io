38 /**
39  * @brief Atom decomposition cell system.
40  *
41  * This implements a distributed particle storage
42  * by just evenly distributing the particles over
43  * all part-taking nodes. Pairs are found by just
44  * considering all pairs independent of logical or
45  * physical location, it has therefore quadratic time
46  * complexity in the number of particles.
47  *
48  * For a more detailed discussion please see @cite plimpton1995a.
49  */
