Detailed Description

Routines to calculate the generalized Lennard-Jones potential between particle pairs. "Generalized" here means that the LJ energy is of the form

\[ \varepsilon \cdot \left[ b_1 \left(\frac{\sigma}{r-r_{\text{offset}}}\right)^{a_1} - b_2 \left(\frac{\sigma}{r-r_{\text{offset}}}\right)^{a_2} + \text{shift} \right] \]
