◆ neutralize

bool ELCParameters::neutralize

Flag whether the box is neutralized by a homogeneous background.

If true, use a homogeneous neutralizing background for nonneutral systems. Unlike the 3D case, this background adds an additional force pointing towards the system center, so be careful with this.
