Detailed Description

main header-file for MDLC (magnetic dipolar layer correction).

Developer: Joan J. Cerda.

Purpose: get the corrections for dipolar 3D algorithms when applied to a slab geometry and dipolar particles. DLC & co

Article: [8]

We also include a tuning function that returns the cut-off necessary to attend a certain accuracy.

Restrictions: the slab must be such that the z is the short direction. Otherwise we get trash.

Limitations: at this moment it is restricted to work with 1 cpu
