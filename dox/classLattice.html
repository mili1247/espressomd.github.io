Map a spatial position to the surrounding lattice sites.

This function takes a global spatial position and determines the surrounding elementary cell of the lattice for this position. The distance fraction in each direction is also calculated.
Remarks:

  • The spatial position has to be in the local domain.
  • The lattice sites of the elementary cell are returned as local indices
Parameters
posspatial position (Input)
node_indexlocal indices of the surrounding lattice sites (Output)
deltadistance fraction of p pos from the surrounding elementary cell, 6 directions (Output)
