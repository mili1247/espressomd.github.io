23 /** \file
24  * All 3d non-P3M methods to deal with the magnetic dipoles
25  *
26  * DAWAANR => Dipolar All With All And No Replica
27  * Handling of a system of dipoles where no replicas exist.
28  * Assumes minimum image convention for those axis in which the
29  * system is periodic.
30  *
31  * MDDS => Magnetic Dipoles Direct Sum
32  * Calculate dipole-dipole interaction of a periodic system
33  * by explicitly summing the dipole-dipole interaction over several copies of
34  * the system.
35  * Uses spherical summation order.
36  *
37  */
